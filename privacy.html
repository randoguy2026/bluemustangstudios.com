Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

Blue Mustang Studios ("we," "us," or "our") is a small independent game studio based in the United States. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our games, websites, and related services (the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide: If you email us, submit a bug report, or contact support, we collect whatever information you include in that message (like your name or email address).

Automatically Collected Information: Our games and website may automatically collect basic data such as:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Crash logs and basic diagnostics
  • Gameplay data (progress, session length) to help us improve the game

App Store Information: If you make a purchase, the app store (Apple or Google) handles payment. We may receive a confirmation that a purchase was made, but we do not see or store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Run and improve our games and services
  • Respond to support requests
  • Fix bugs and diagnose crashes
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

3. Sharing

We do not share your personal information except:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors that help us run our services (such as hosting or analytics).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has sent us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To make a request, email us at the address below.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here with a new effective date.
